## What is Nezha Mobile

Nezha Mobile is an unofficial client for [Nezha Dashboard](https://nezha.wiki), bringing server monitoring to the entire Apple ecosystem. Watch your servers, view real-time metrics, manage alert rules, and get push notifications — wherever you are.

## Platforms

Nezha Mobile is a universal app built natively for every Apple platform:

— iPhone & iPad (iOS / iPadOS 17.0+)
— Mac (macOS 14.0+)
— Apple Watch (watchOS 10.0+)
— Apple Vision Pro (visionOS 1.0+)

## Features

### Monitor your fleet in real time

- **Live server list** — CPU, memory, swap, disk, and inbound/outbound network speeds for every node, refreshing as it happens.
- **Deep server detail** — load averages, uptime, process count, geolocation, and historical charts so you can drill in the moment something looks off.
- **Servers on the map** — your entire fleet plotted on a world map with city-level grouping, ideal for spotting regional outages at a glance.

### Catch issues before they catch you

- **Alert rules** — create, edit, and tune thresholds for CPU, memory, disk, traffic, and more, all from your phone.
- **Service monitoring** — track uptime and availability of HTTP, TCP, and ICMP services with history at a tap.
- **Push notifications** — APNs delivery lights up your iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch the moment a rule trips.

### Manage everything on the go

- **Notifications & notification groups** — wire up your delivery channels and route alerts to the right place without ever leaving the app.
- **Server groups** — organize servers by environment, region, or customer — your structure, your way.
- **Snippets** — save commands and config blocks once, then reuse them anywhere with a tap.

### Built native, end to end

- **Widgets everywhere** — fleet summary, per-server detail, and Agent widgets across Home Screen, Lock Screen, StandBy, and the Mac desktop, with one-tap refresh via App Intents.
- **iCloud sync** — sign in once; your dashboard credentials and preferences follow you to every device, with secrets safely stored in Keychain.
- **Themes** — fluid SwiftUI design that respects your light, dark, and accent preferences.
